# Privacy Policy for Caarxd

**Last Updated:** [INSERT DATE]

## Introduction

Caarxd ("we," "our," or "us") respects your privacy and is committed to protecting your personal data. This privacy policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

## Information We Collect

### Information You Provide Directly

When you use Caarxd, you may provide us with:

- **Account Information:** Name, email address, phone number, password
- **Business Card Data:** Names, job titles, company names, contact information, logos, QR codes
- **Profile Information:** Profile photos, personal details you choose to share

### Automatically Collected Information

- **Usage Data:** How you interact with the app, features used, time spent
- **Device Information:** Device type, operating system version, unique device identifiers
- **Analytics Data:** Card views, shares, and engagement metrics (anonymized)

### Information from Third Parties

- **Firebase Authentication:** Authentication tokens and user IDs (Google/Firebase)
- **Photo Library:** Images you select for card logos (with your permission)
- **Contacts:** When you export cards to iOS Contacts (with your permission)
- **Camera:** When scanning QR codes or business cards (with your permission)

## How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

1. **Provide Services**
   - Create and manage your digital business cards
   - Generate QR codes and shareable links
   - Track card performance and analytics
   - Sync your data across devices

2. **Improve the App**
   - Analyze usage patterns to enhance features
   - Fix bugs and technical issues
   - Develop new features based on user needs

3. **Security**
   - Protect against fraud and abuse
   - Verify user identity
   - Secure your account and data

4. **Communication**
   - Send service updates and notifications
   - Respond to your questions and support requests
   - Share important security or policy updates

## Data Storage and Security

### Where We Store Data

- **Firebase Cloud Firestore:** Business cards, analytics, user profiles
- **Local Device Storage:** Cached data for offline access (SwiftData)
- **Firebase Authentication:** Encrypted authentication credentials

### How We Protect Data

- **Encryption:** All data is encrypted in transit (HTTPS/TLS) and at rest
- **Access Controls:** Strict user authentication and authorization
- **Regular Security Audits:** We review and update security measures regularly
- **Secure Infrastructure:** Firebase/Google Cloud Platform security standards

### Data Retention

- **Active Accounts:** Data retained while your account is active
- **Deleted Accounts:** Data permanently deleted within 30 days of account deletion
- **Analytics:** Aggregated, anonymized analytics retained for up to 2 years

## Data Sharing and Disclosure

### We DO NOT Sell Your Data

We will never sell your personal information to third parties.

### Limited Sharing

We may share your information only in these circumstances:

1. **With Your Consent:** When you explicitly share business cards with others
2. **Service Providers:** Firebase/Google Cloud (infrastructure only)
3. **Legal Requirements:** If required by law, court order, or government request
4. **Business Transfer:** In case of merger, acquisition, or asset sale (users notified)
5. **Safety:** To protect rights, property, or safety of Caarxd, users, or public

### Public Information

Information you choose to share publicly:
- Shared business cards (via QR codes or links)
- Information visible on cards you distribute

## Your Privacy Rights

### Access and Control

You have the right to:

- **Access:** View all data we have about you
- **Update:** Edit your profile and business card information
- **Delete:** Request deletion of your account and data
- **Export:** Download your data in portable format
- **Opt-Out:** Disable analytics tracking (in Settings)

### How to Exercise Your Rights

- **In-App:** Settings → Profile → Manage Data
- **Email:** privacy@caarxd.app (or your actual email)
- **Response Time:** We'll respond within 30 days

## Children's Privacy

- **Right to Rectification:** Correct inaccurate data
- **Right to Erasure:** "Right to be forgotten"

- **Right to Data Portability:** Receive your data in machine-readable format
- **Right to Object:** Object to data processing
- **Right to Lodge Complaint:** File complaint with supervisory authority

To exercise these rights, contact: privacy@caarxd.app
